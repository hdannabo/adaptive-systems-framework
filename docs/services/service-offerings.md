# ASF Service Offerings — Engagement Model

> **For business development conversations.** This document covers the three-tier engagement model, what each tier delivers, who it is for, and how ASF and the implementation LLC work together.

---

## What ASF Delivers

ASF is a diagnostic and intelligence platform. The LLC is the implementation partner. The two operate as an integrated offer:

**ASF answers:** What is blocking capability realization, how long will it take to close the gap, and which technology intervention compresses that timeline most?

**The LLC delivers:** Cloud modernization, AI agents, data platforms, ERP/workflow automation, DevOps/MLOps, predictive maintenance, executive dashboards, supply chain analytics, and security/governance — prioritized by the diagnostic output.

This is the key differentiator: technology investment is sequenced and prioritized by evidence, not by vendor preference or technology novelty.

---

## The Three Engagement Tiers

### Tier 1 — Executive Diagnostic

**Investment:** ₹15–25 Lakhs  
**Timeline:** 4 weeks  
**Format:** Structured engagement with leadership team + access to internal programme data

**Who it is for:**  
CEO or MD who wants an independent, evidence-backed read on whether a strategic programme is on track before board reporting reflects it. CFO or investor who wants to understand capital deployment risk against realistic capability timelines. New leadership taking over a complex programme who needs a baseline diagnostic.

**What we do in 4 weeks:**

| Week | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 1 | Observation Layer: collect and classify internal programme data, operational metrics, leadership priorities |
| 2 | Capability Model: 10-dimension assessment scored against internal data. EFS derivation. |
| 3 | CRT Engine: CRT per strategic objective. Dependency Risk Map. Binding constraint identification. |
| 4 | Executive output: CEO-format dashboard, Intervention Roadmap, Prediction Register issuance |

**What you receive:**

- 10-dimension capability assessment, evidence-linked
- CRT estimate per objective with confidence intervals narrowed to ±12–15%
- Named binding constraint — the one action that most improves your timeline
- Dependency Risk Map across six dimensions with mitigation recommendations
- CEO-format decision dashboard (HTML, live-updatable)
- Intervention Roadmap: 3 priority actions, sequenced by CRT impact
- Timestamped prediction register — publicly tracked, not privately held
- 30-day follow-on access for clarification and board preparation

**What is not included:** Technology implementation. The diagnostic identifies what to do and why. Implementation is a separate engagement (Tier 3).

---

### Tier 2 — Transformation Roadmap

**Investment:** ₹50–80 Lakhs  
**Timeline:** 8 weeks  
**Format:** Full programme engagement, multi-objective scope

**Who it is for:**  
Leadership team managing multiple simultaneous strategic programmes (e.g., Amara Raja with EV + BESS + LAB simultaneously). CTO or CDO who needs to justify technology investment sequencing to the board. Transformation head who needs a quantified action-value case for the intervention roadmap.

**What Tier 2 adds over Tier 1:**

- Multi-programme CRT modelling — all strategic objectives assessed simultaneously
- Resource conflict analysis — identifies where two programmes are competing for the same management bandwidth, technology capacity, or talent pool
- Technology intervention scoring — which IT investment (cloud, MES, AI quality, data platform, DevOps) compresses CRT most, and by how much
- Scenario modelling — CRT impact of each major leadership decision (e.g., "What happens to the EV programme CRT if we sequence BESS second?")
- Board-ready presentation with quantified action value per recommendation
- Implementation partner brief for each identified technology intervention
- Extended prediction register covering all programmes in scope

**Deliverable format:** Full executive report + scenario workbook + board presentation draft + implementation briefs for cloud/AI/data/automation interventions.

---

### Tier 3 — Implementation Partnership

**Investment:** ₹10–18 Lakhs / quarter  
**Timeline:** Ongoing quarterly engagement  
**Format:** Retainer with quarterly CRT refresh and implementation support

**Who it is for:**  
Leadership teams who want ASF intelligence maintained as a live operational asset, not a one-time diagnostic. Companies in active technology transition who need both the diagnostic intelligence and the technology implementation — together, rather than through a series of disconnected vendors.

**What a quarterly retainer includes:**

- **Quarterly CRT refresh:** Updated capability assessment as programme data evolves. Confidence intervals narrow as implementation progresses and internal data improves.
- **Live capability dashboard:** HTML dashboard updated for board and CXO meetings. Objective confidence, CRT, EFS, and dependency risk tracked in real time.
- **Prediction register maintenance:** Each issued prediction tracked against outcomes. New predictions issued as programme milestones approach.
- **Early-warning signal review:** Non-financial leading indicators monitored monthly. Regulatory delays, technology partner risk signals, and talent risk signals surfaced before they appear in financial reporting.
- **Technology implementation support:** Direct delivery or oversight of cloud modernization, AI quality analytics, MES deployment, supply chain analytics, DevOps platform build, and executive dashboard development — prioritized by diagnostic output from each quarterly cycle.
- **On-call executive intelligence:** Access between quarterly cycles for decisions that cannot wait for the next refresh. Response within 48 hours on programme-level questions.

**Retainer does NOT include:**  
Third-party software licenses, cloud infrastructure costs, hardware, or third-party data subscriptions. These are pass-through costs at cost.

## What ASF Does Not Do

**ASF does not:**

- Fabricate client names, revenue, or ROI claims. Every reference in public materials is either a named public company with cited public data, or explicitly hypothetical.
- Make revenue guarantees or ROI commitments. CRT estimates and intervention impact assessments are analytical outputs, not commercial warranties.
- Replace legal, financial, or regulatory advice. CRT estimates that involve regulatory timelines are based on historical patterns and publicly stated regulatory processes — not legal counsel.
- Deliver a one-size-fits-all framework output. Every engagement uses the same methodology, but every diagnostic is built from the client's specific data and context.

## Engagement Entry Points

**"We have a specific programme we need clarity on"** → Tier 1 Executive Diagnostic. Start with the binding constraint.

**"We have multiple strategic programmes and need to prioritize"** → Tier 2 Transformation Roadmap. Multi-objective CRT with resource conflict analysis.

**"We need ongoing intelligence and technology delivery"** → Tier 3 Implementation Partnership. Quarterly retainer with implementation support.

**"We're not sure what we need"** → 30-minute briefing using public data for your company. No preparation required. If the diagnostic framework adds value, you'll know in the first session.
