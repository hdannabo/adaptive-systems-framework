## Section 4: Cross-Cutting Evidence

### E-CROSS-01: Antimony Supply — Chinese Export Control
- **Fact:** China controls ~80% of global antimony supply. China restricted antimony exports in mid-2024. This caused 5x price surge affecting all Indian LAB manufacturers.
- **Confidence:** HIGH — corroborated by commodity market trackers and company earnings
- **Used in:** Dependency Risk (both AR and Exide); Risk Heatmap

### E-CROSS-02: India EV Battery PLI Scheme
- **Fact:** India's PLI for Advanced Chemistry Cell (ACC) Battery Manufacturing offers ₹18,100 crore incentive for 50 GWh of domestic manufacturing by 2030. Both Amara Raja and Exide have applied/been selected.
- **Source:** Ministry of Heavy Industries, 2021; subsequent updates
- **Confidence:** MEDIUM — PLI scheme selection widely reported; disbursement terms and current status require more specific verification
- **Used in:** Strategic Clarity background; Chinese technology dependency assessment context

### E-CROSS-03: Indian LAB Market Size
- **Fact:** Indian LAB market approximately ₹25,000 crore in FY25
- **Confidence:** MEDIUM — this is market sizing from analyst reports; not directly cited in any primary source reviewed
- **Status:** Used for context only; not scored against this figure
