Section 7 · How ASF Works

From Signal Collection to Executive Decision

ASF is a structured analytical methodology — not a software platform. Each layer represents a disciplined analytical process. Platform automation of individual layers is the roadmap for each engagement.

1
Signal Collection
Regulatory filings, credit reports, investor presentations, exchange disclosures
›
2
Capability Mapping
10-dimension scoring — current vs required capability per strategic objective
›
3
Dependency Risk
Six-dimension external risk classification — what is outside operational control

4
CRT Estimation
Time-to-capability estimate with explicit confidence intervals
›
5
Intervention Ranking
Actions ranked by estimated CRT impact — not by technology preference
›
6
Implementation
Cloud, AI, data, automation, DevOps — delivered by the LLC
›
7
Outcome Tracking
Prediction validation and quarterly CRT refresh as programme evolves
What internal data changes: Public data provides the diagnostic framework (Steps 1–5 above) with ±25–35% CI. Internal programme data — yield rates, leadership decision logs, production schedules, resource plans — replaces MEDIUM-confidence items with HIGH-confidence operational data, narrows CI to ±15–20%, and makes the CRT estimate operationally precise rather than directionally indicative.
